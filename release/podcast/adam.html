    Adam Shabbir on Lean Living and Play Time
    19/10/15
    Adam Shabbir is a startup sansei. He shares decades of experience in and around Old Street, comparing startup mannerisms from then and now.

    Adam is a huge believer in lean development and empowering developers. His fluid ability to manage products is futuristic, flat and fun.

    He leads an international lifestyle, jetting to dozens of countries a year with his family. He believes a holiday should tire you out more than work.

    A true gentleman and vet in the tech world.
