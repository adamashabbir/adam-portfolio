    COMMON PENCE
    PROJECT WEBSITE
    Innovation in donation
    Common Pence is a donation platform utilising Contactless Credit Cards and NFC Smart Phones. The concept combines contemporary design with modern technology to revolutionise fundraising.
    WHY
    IDEA
    Traditionally, our desire to donate has been accomplished with cash. However, as our research and open source trends suggest the demand for cash is fading.Opposite to this is the growth in contactless payment.
    TECH
    In a world restricted by time and unrestricted by technology, it is our aim to ensure that giving is effortless. Tap, whack, wave or flick your device on our panels to donate change to a great cause.
    PROJECTS
    We are keen to assist creative ventures and adventures. From Museums to startups, we want to help fund those preserving and those making history. The convenience of tapping plastic really is fantastic!
    WHAT
    EASY
    Cash is rapidly being replaced by contactless payment. Common Pence, encourages people to act and not think.
    DATA
    Unlike cash donations, Common Pence can map donation hot spots. This allows charities to match their user needs.
    FLEXIBLE
    Common Pence's handheld, hanging and point of sale devices offers ultimate flexibility for charity campaigns.
