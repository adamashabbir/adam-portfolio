    Zander undertook his Masters with a Fellowship Scholarship. Combining his 3D, 2D and UX design skills with technology expanded his observation for innovation.     Studying at the University of Oxford, Christ Church College, provided a wonderful opportunity for Zander to broaden his creative and intellectual aptitude. During his degree he exhibited in the Saatchi Gallery for the World Schools Art prize in 2010. He developed an appreciation for Honest critique which he believes is irreplaceable in a creative environment. His passion for inventive design was influenced by brilliant tutorship. Balancing his degree with sporting and social commitments, Zander represented the Oxford Blues 1st XI Football team. As an Undergraduate at Oxford University, Christ Church, his core area of interest was location based media and his dissertation discussed the utopian potentials of ubiquitous computing in the urban environment.
