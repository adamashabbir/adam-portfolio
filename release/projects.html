    H@ - Emotive Mapping
    How does our brain respond to the city? H@ is a solar powered, EEG and GPS wearable. As a 3D printed wearable, H@ detects brainwaves, measuring attentive and meditative states, which are interlinked in real-time with Longitude and Latitude to generate an emotive map of any city.
